This is a simple cookies and cream milkshake recipe that's good for starters first getting into recipes. With minimal instructions and ingredients, the taste is definitely worth it!

Ingredients

  • 2-4 scoops of cookies and cream ice cream
  • 1-2 cups of milk

Directions

  1. Add contents to blender
    • Add ice cream in blender
    • Add milk in blender
  2. Blender contents prefered amount ~30 seconds

Amount of scoops of ice cream and cups of milk is up to preference, depending on how much liquid and chunk contents you'd like. Blending amount is also up to preference. liquid and solid chunks. Optionally, you can also include whip cream and a cherry on the top. ðŸ˜‹
