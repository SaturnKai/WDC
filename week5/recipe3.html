For our entree, we have the mighty crispy chicken sandwich, a certified classic. üòé

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1 box of cornflakes

  • 1 box of cornflakes
  • 2 brioche buns
  • 2 slices of tomatos
  • 2 cups of lettuce
  • 3 pickles
  • 2 cups of mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Wisk eggs and milk in a bowl
  2. Create cornflake mixture
    • Mix cornflakes and prefered seasonings in a bag
    • Crush mixture into a crumb like result
  3. Apply cornflake breading to chicken breast
    • Dip chicken breast into egg mixture
    • Coat cornflake mixture onto breast
  4. Cook chicken breast
    • Place coated breast in air fryer
    • Fry at 400 degrees for 10-15 minutes
  5. Create sandwich
    • Apply mayonaise on both sides of buns
    • Place cooked breast onto buns
    • Place pickles, lettuce and tomatoes

The cornflakes provide a crunchy texture for the outside, and depending on the chicken breast size, you can apply any amount of breading as you'd like. It's recommended to run the airfryer for at least 8 minutes on high to ensure it's ready before cooking the breast.
