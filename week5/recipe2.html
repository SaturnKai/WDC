Another simple recipe, a cheese quesadilla! The cheese in combination with the toasted tortillas pack together to create this hot but juicy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 4 cups of shredded cheese
  • 1 scoop of sour cream with chives

Directions

  1. Apply one tortilla completely flat to stovetop
  2. Add contents on top of tortilla.
    • Apply the cheese and make sure to spread evenly
    • Add sour cream towards to center of tortilla
  3. Place the other tortilla on top of the one on the stovetop
  4. Toast until golden brown
    • Flip halfway through cooking to ensure both sides are cooked

Amount of cheese and sour cream depends on tortilla size. While toasting of tortillas is not required, the extra crisp definitely makes it worth the while!
